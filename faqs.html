Gedhe Endhase is a sustainable fashion brand that transforms coffee waste into premium, biodegradable materials like coffee leather. Our mission is to redefine luxury fashion by combining innovation, environmental responsibility, and ethical practices.

Coffee leather is a plant-based material crafted from discarded coffee grounds and husks. Through a proprietary process, we blend coffee waste with natural binders to create a durable, flexible, and eco-friendly alternative to animal or synthetic leather. It’s lightweight, antibacterial, and free from toxic chemicals.

Yes! Our coffee leather undergoes rigorous testing to ensure it matches the durability of conventional leather. It’s resistant to wear and tear, water-repellent, and designed to age gracefully. Our coffee leather have 13.25 MPa Rating which is superior to many traditional animal and synthetic leathers. Plus, it’s gentler on the planet—100% biodegradable at the end of its lifecycle.

  • Waste Reduction: We upcycle coffee waste that would otherwise end up in landfills.
  • Carbon Footprint: Our production emits 70% less CO₂ compared to traditional synthetic leather.

Absolutely! Our materials are 100% plant-based and cruelty-free. No animals are harmed in our production process.

We partner with local coffee farms and cafes across Indonesia, ensuring fair compensation for farmers. We source our coffee leather from The Bell Society where their M-Tex Leather meets our standards. This collaboration supports rural economies and reduces waste at the source.

  • Fair wages and working conditions: Artisans and workers receive the best welfare we could offer.
  • Safe Conditions: Factories adhere to strict environmental and labor standards.
  • Transparency: We share stories of our farmers and artisans to connect you with the hands behind your product.
